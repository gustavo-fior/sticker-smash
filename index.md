## The Technical Layer Between Design and Engineering

Figma bridges the gap between human creativity and AI-driven development. By leveraging **Figma Make** and **Dev Mode**, teams can eliminate context loss during handoff and accelerate product development.

* **Model Context Protocol (MCP) Integration:** Connect Figma directly to your AI agents and IDEs. The Figma MCP Server ensures LLMs have real-time access to atomic components and design tokens.
* **Figma Make for Co-Creation:** Act as an iteration partner, not a replacement. Prompt, generate, and refine designs using your existing design systems to balance speed with thoughtful exploration.
* **Dev Mode Handoff:** Translate designs into production-ready code with technical accuracy, preserving the structural integrity of your UI/UX.
